📚 How Walk-Forward Works
Step 1: Split full data into N folds (e.g., 5 folds of 2 months each)
Step 2: Train strategy on fold 1-3 (in-sample), test on fold 4 (out-of-sample)
Step 3: Roll forward — train on fold 2-4, test on fold 5
Step 4: Compare in-sample vs out-of-sample performance
Rule: If OOS win rate < IS win rate − 10% → strategy is overfit ❌
Rule: If OOS Sharpe > 1.0 consistently → strategy is robust ✔

🧠 How Q-Learning Works
State: VIX level, Market regime, Win streak, Drawdown level (256 states)
Actions: TRADE / NO-TRADE / REDUCE_SIZE (3 actions)
Reward: +profit, −loss, −2×drawdown (tunable)
Goal: Agent learns WHEN to trade vs when to stay out
Q-update: Q(s,a) ← Q(s,a) + α[r + γ·max Q(s',a') − Q(s,a)]
